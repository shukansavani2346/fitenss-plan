Three Pillars of Success
Train Smart & Recover

PPL 6 days, progressive overload, sleep 7-8 hrs minimum. Muscle is built during recovery, not in the gym.

Vegetarian Surplus

At 56 kg you likely eat under 2,200 kcal/day. You need 2,700–3,000 kcal to grow. Pure veg fits this easily with planning.

Skin & Hydration

80% skin clarity is gut health and hydration. Keep your garlic empty-stomach habit, and add mild sunscreen.

PPL Split vs. Double Split
Your current split is solid. However, PPL (Push-Pull-Legs) organizes workouts by movement patterns rather than isolation, reducing muscle overlap and optimizing joint recovery. PPL is highly recommended for building structural volume to move from 56 → 70 kg. If you stick with the double split, ensure recovery times are kept consistent.

Overload & Rest Protocols
Top of Rep Range Rule

When you hit the maximum reps (e.g. 8 reps in a 6-8 range) with good form for 2 consecutive sessions, increase the weight by 2.5 kg next time.

Rest Intervals

Rest 2–3 minutes for heavy compound lifts (squat, bench, deadlift). Rest 60–90 seconds for accessory/isolation lifts.
