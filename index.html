PPL Split vs. Double Split
Your current split is solid. However, PPL (Push-Pull-Legs) organizes workouts by movement patterns rather than isolation, reducing muscle overlap and optimizing joint recovery. PPL is highly recommended for building structural volume to move from 56 → 70 kg. If you stick with the double split, ensure recovery times are kept consistent.
